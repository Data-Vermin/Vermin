I'm Eris Thorn, my aliases are Samantha Foxx, and Data Vermin. As a polymath and ADHD artist based in Portland Oregon, my creative journey is a tapestry woven from diverse threads of storytelling, world-building, and interactive experiences. My work spans multiple mediums, including digital art, coding, music, and game design, allowing me to explore the interconnectedness of these forms in innovative ways.

I am passionate about conceptual art and product design, where whimsy meets functionality. My hands-on approach, using techniques like laser cutting and object printing, allows me to turn ideas into tangible forms, exploring how art shapes our understanding and fosters connection.

Ultimately, my work reflects a commitment to discovery, embracing the chaos of creativity while celebrating the joy of building new worlds. Through each project, I aim to invite others into a shared narrative, creating spaces where imagination thrives.
